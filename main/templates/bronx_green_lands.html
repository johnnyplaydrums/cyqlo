Bronx Green Lands

Explore the Bronx like never before. This route emphasizes the green parts of the Bronx which are an eye opener. Experience the historic scenery of Van Cortlandt's House Museum. Then travel to the beautiful New York Botonic Gardens. Just a short distance away is the largest metropolitan zoo in the United States, The Bronx Zoo. Next enjoy biking through Pelham Bay Park to the historic Bartow-Pell Mansion Museum. Lastly, conclude the bike route with some nice and relaxing breezes on the boardwalk of Orchard Beach. If ever in a jiffy, there are bike shops along the way!
