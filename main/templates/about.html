CREATING A WORLD WHERE EVERYONE BIKES TO WORK, BIKES TO EXPLORE, AND BIKES FOR FUN.

SAFETY

As a team who loves to bike, we know how trecherous biking around and out of the city can be. That's exactly why we've built Cyqlo: To ease the pain for all types of cyclists. We're tackling the problem of real-time road conditions, to let cyclists unobtrusively know when there are hazardous conditions along their path and when they're approaching notoriously dangerous intersections.

NAVIGATION

Knowing where to go is a necessary component no matter the mode of travel. When biking, the odds seem a little higher as a misstep, or innacurate directions can lead you on a not so pleasant journey. Road closures and construction add chaos and uncertainty to biking, and we're making sure our riders are armed with the information to make their journeys more comfortable and well-informed.

EXPLORATION

Whether you haven't biked in years, you're new to a city, or your same old routes are getting stale, Cyqlo wants to help users find and explore new places. We're leverging technology to help reconnect people with nature and helping them discover the beauty in their own backyard, or somewhere completely new. We love the feeling of finding hidden gems and beauty in unexpected places and we want to share that with our riders.
